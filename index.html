About Me

My name is Marte Rodriguez

Creating something and solving problems-- these are the two things I am most passionate about.

Ever since I was a kid, I have always enjoyed building Lego's and transforming robots. As I grew up, I aspired to be a writer, an animator, or a video game developer, dreaming to see my characters come to life.

After taking a two year degree for Web App and Cloud Development at Edmonds College, I instantly fell in love with coding. I enjoy what I build every time I code something and something pops up. I enjoy debugging problems that arise as I go through problems. I thoroughly look forward to new things I learn everyday.
